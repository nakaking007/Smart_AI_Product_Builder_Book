# Canonical Scope — Smart AI Product Builder Complete Edition

**Decision status:** Approved canonical version

**Effective date:** 19 July 2026
**Supersedes:** BASIC day-one-only outline and six-book-first production plan

## Canonical Product

The primary book is **Smart AI Product Builder — Complete Edition**, a single-volume, two-day course book delivered as one PDF.

The historical directory name `SERIES/01_BASIC` is retained to avoid breaking links and Git history. It does not limit the approved content scope.

## Delivery Rule

- Primary deliverable: `Smart_AI_Product_Builder_Complete_Edition_v1.0.pdf`
- All chapters, appendices, examples, references, and index are combined into this one PDF.
- Markdown, code, image, DOCX, and render files are production sources or QA artifacts.
- The PDF is not considered complete until content, technical, visual, and print preflight checks pass.
- The print edition is a full-color commercial computer book using the approved B5 working grid, CMYK production, 300 PPI imagery, and original premium artwork.
- Every content page uses full-color page furniture, an outer-edge section menu, semantic alternating heading colors, and at least one useful visual anchor.
- Ribbon navigation uses eight approved Part colors; supplementary Project, Workshop, and Appendix tabs preserve all canonical content. People illustrations use authentic contemporary Thai contexts.

## Extended Series Status

Books 2–6 are a future **Professional Extended Series**. They may reuse and expand canonical content, but they do not replace or block production of the Complete Edition.

## Two-Day Teaching Constraint

The book is a complete reference; the instructor does not read every page in class. The two-day learning route prioritizes guided demonstrations, workshops, and one working Capstone. Deep reference sections remain available for self-study and later application.

## Change Control

Any future change that removes one of the nine required course subjects, Single Web App, Workshop, Capstone, Prompt Library, or appendices requires explicit approval from the author and a new decision record.
