# Content Plan

This file defines chapter intent only. It is not chapter manuscript content.

## Part I — AI foundations and interaction

### Chapter 1 — AI Foundation

Establish essential AI concepts, capabilities, limitations, responsible use, and a practical product-builder mindset.

### Chapter 2 — Prompt Engineering

Introduce structured prompting, context, constraints, examples, evaluation, iteration, and reusable prompt patterns.

## Part II — Configurable AI tools

### Chapter 3 — Gem

Explain how to design and evaluate a focused Gemini Gem for a repeatable personal or business task.

### Chapter 4 — Custom GPT

Cover the planning, configuration, knowledge, instructions, testing, and safe sharing of a Custom GPT.

### Chapter 5 — NotebookLM

Show how to build source-grounded research and knowledge workflows while checking citations and source quality.

## Part III — Lightweight product building

### Chapter 6 — HTML Single File

Introduce the web-page building blocks needed to create a portable, interactive tool in one HTML file.

### Chapter 7 — Google Apps Script

Build practical Google Workspace automations and web apps with permissions, testing, and deployment explained clearly.

### Chapter 8 — GAS Project Generator

Design a structured generator that turns requirements into a maintainable Google Apps Script project rather than isolated snippets.

### Chapter 9 — Chrome Extension

Explain extension architecture, permissions, user experience, local testing, packaging, security, and publication readiness.

## Part IV — AI-assisted product delivery

### Chapter 10 — Codex

Teach effective collaboration with Codex across planning, repository work, implementation, verification, and handoff.

### Chapter 11 — AI Product

Combine product discovery, user needs, data, model choice, interface design, evaluation, safety, cost, release, and improvement into an end-to-end framework.

### Chapter 12 — Appendix

Collect the glossary, checklists, troubleshooting guidance, reusable prompt patterns, technical references, and resource index.

## Cross-chapter progression

- Chapters 1–2 build shared vocabulary and reliable interaction habits.
- Chapters 3–5 apply those habits in configurable AI products.
- Chapters 6–9 introduce increasingly capable product containers.
- Chapters 10–11 establish a professional build-and-release workflow.
- Chapter 12 provides reusable reference material.

## Planned supporting materials

- Reusable prompt cards under `prompts/`
- Tested code examples under `code/`
- Worksheets and checklists under `templates/`
- Source records under `references/`
- Diagrams, screenshots, icons, and QR codes under `assets/`
